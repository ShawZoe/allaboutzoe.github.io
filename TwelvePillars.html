    Twelve Pillars Skills

Summary

What it will take for you to achieve your goal is the main justification for setting one. Therefore, setting challenging goals and creating a plan to get there are necessary if you want to achieve your desired future. Spend your time on the things that matter most and the situations that are most conducive to achieving your objectives. Spend time with successful people who live lives that are aligned with the values and abilities you want to cultivate. Finally, you should self-educate, which entails learning for yourself and using what you discover along the way to keep growing and improving.

How Can I Apply These to my Life?

  1. Create meaningful long term goals for my future.
  2. Make the best of each important opportunity everyday.
  3. Make friends with not only like minded people, but also people that can push me to be the best version of myself.
  4. Take the time to learn from my life experiences.
